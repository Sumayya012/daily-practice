Aloo Paratha

Soft whole-wheat flatbread stuffed with spiced mashed potatoes, perfect for breakfast or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups wheat flour
  • 2 medium potatoes (boiled & mashed)
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp red chilli powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander
  • Oil or ghee for cooking

Instructions

  1. Prepare dough with wheat flour, water, and a pinch of salt.
  2. Mix mashed potatoes with spices, salt, and coriander.
  3. Roll small dough balls, fill with potato mixture, seal, and roll again.
  4. Cook on a hot tawa with oil/ghee until golden on both sides.
  5. Serve hot with curd or pickle.
